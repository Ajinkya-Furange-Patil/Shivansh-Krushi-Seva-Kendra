Return & Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping at Shivansh Krushi Seva Kendra. We strive to provide quality farming and agricultural products. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Return Eligibility

You may request a return under the following conditions, in accordance with Indian consumer laws:

  • The item must be unused and in the same condition you received it.
  • The item must be in the original packaging.
  • The return request must be initiated within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items like seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, and perishable items (e.g., fresh produce) are not eligible for return unless damaged or defective.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Perishable goods such as plants, fresh fruits, vegetables, etc.
  • Customized or special-order items.
  • Opened or used items like seeds, pesticides, or fertilizers.

Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a product that is damaged or defective, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with images or video proof. We will either replace the item or initiate a full refund as per our discretion.

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer support at furangeaditya@gmail.com +91 9011102731 with your order number and reason for return.
  2. We will arrange for pickup or guide you to ship the item back.
  3. After we inspect the product, we will notify you of the status of your refund or replacement.

Refunds

If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. The credit will reflect within 7–10 business days depending on your bank.

Legal Compliance

This policy is in accordance with the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 and the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020, as applicable in India. You are entitled to fair and transparent return and refund processes as a consumer.
