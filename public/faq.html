Support & Frequently Asked Questions

Need help? Find answers to common questions about our farming products, shipping, and support.

1. What types of farming products do you sell?

We offer a wide range of farming supplies including high-yield seeds, organic and chemical fertilizers, pest control solutions, farming tools, irrigation equipment, and protective gear for farmers.

2. Are your seeds certified and non-GMO?

Yes, all our seeds are certified by agricultural authorities and are non-GMO unless otherwise clearly stated on the product label.

3. How do I choose the right fertilizer for my crops?

Each crop has different nutrient requirements. We provide detailed product descriptions and crop recommendations on every fertilizer page. You can also contact our agronomy support team for free advice.

4. Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship across the India. For bulk international orders, please contact our support team directly for custom shipping solutions.

5. How long will delivery take?

Orders are processed within 3–4 working days. Standard shipping usually takes 3–5 working days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

6. Can I return a product if it doesn’t work for my farm?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy on most products. The product must be unused and in original packaging. Please check our Return Policy for full details.

7. Do you offer bulk discounts for large farms?

Yes, we offer special pricing and discounts for bulk orders. Contact our sales team at furangeaditya@gmail.com for a custom quote.

8. How can I get technical support for using a product?

You can reach our support team via email, phone, or live chat. We also provide manuals, video tutorials, and expert advice on product pages.

9. Do you have eco-friendly or organic-certified products?

Yes, we stock a range of organic-certified products including compost, bio-pesticides, and natural growth boosters. Look for the “Organic Certified” badge on product listings.
